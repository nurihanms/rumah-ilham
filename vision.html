Vision & Mission

1. Provides shelter, counseling, and education to orphans.

2. Create a generation of orphans balance between the spiritual and physical demands.

3. Makes generation of Muslims who are not only knowledgeable and do even implies it to the general public.

4. Generation of Muslims who are competent and charismatic in all fields of endeavor.

Tazkirah

Prophet Muhammad also said: The one who keeps the orphan, (i) the Prophet and his two fingers like in paradise. The Prophet said, pointing two fingers of his index and middle fingers. (Sahih Muslim)
