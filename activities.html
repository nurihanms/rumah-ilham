Activities

Annual Activities

1. Nasyid present at the celebration of festivals of Islam at Al-Ghufran.

2. Soccer or football match between the dorm and also between home houses nearby orphanage.

3. Hari Raya Open House and the Foundation.

4. Meeting with the mother/guardian and excellent presentation.

5. Family Day Rumah Ilham (staff and children) are usually held outside Kuala Lumpur.

6. Celebrating the orphanage on every 10th of Muharram as organizing sports competitions between the houses orphans.

7. Study tours.

8. Quran recital and Khatam Quran held in every month of Ramadan.

9. Taking part in the 'Gotong-Royong' activities in conjunction with the Hari Raya Korban Masjid Al-Ghufran.

10. Mawlid Celebration participate in the march organized by the Al-Ghufran.


Monthly Activities

Collaborate weekly cleaning in the region and around the foundation.


Weekly Activities

1. Yasin: Every Thursday, Friday and Saturday night after Maghrib prayers in the surau.

2. Request for a religious feast Yasin and only received on Thursday night, Friday and Saturday only.

3. Training nasyid, drums and others.
