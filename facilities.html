  • Dormitory rooms (dorm): There are 3 dorms that can accommodate up to 20 beds in a dorm.
  • Hall Dining: The dining hall tables and chairs placed a set complete with wash hand basin and mirror.
  • Multipurpose Hall: To use the weekly gathering, indoor sports activities such as table tennis, taekwondo class, carom, etc. and dining for functions attended by guests.
  • Place of rest and watching TV: Placing a TV and DVD player for the kids to watch and relax.
  • They prep and tuition: There are 2 rooms and 3 rooms prep tuition.
  • Prayer: Can accommodate about 80 people at any one time.
  • Library & Computer: A place for children to learn and make referrals as well as work involving computers.
  • Place suspension.
  • Laundry Room: Clothing school, formal clothing, blankets and sheets are washed by a washing machine.
  • Transportation (2 buses and a van): Transport is required to attend school, visits and invitations.
